About

I am a PhD candidate in the School of Pharmacy at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. Before coming to UW-Madison, I was an undergraduate at Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani, India where I received degree in Pharmacy (Hons). Working under the mentorship of Prof. Jason Kwan my current research is focused on biosynthesis of natural products from uncultured organisms, algorithm development for metagenomic analysis and studying the construction of diverse communities in soil. Outisde of research I spend my time rock climbing, roller blading and reading on different evolutionary theories.
